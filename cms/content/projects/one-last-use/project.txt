Title: One Last Use

----

Subtitle: a second life for aluminum cans before recycling

----

MainDescription: One last use is a project aimed at turning aluminum beverage cans into recyclable drinking straws. 

Plastic straws are a known pollution problem. They are not recyclable on account of their weight and shape, they find themselves clogging up waterways and the digestive systems of wildlife, and they are made from petrochemicals which keeps a demand to a damaging industry alive.
