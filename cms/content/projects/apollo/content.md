You place a sentimental object on the podium, share its story, and Apollo transforms it into a dynamic LED display, with colors and movements inspired by eight different emotional themes. The design was inspired by ripples in water—how a single moment or story can create waves that spread far beyond itself.

This was a 4-week group project as part of my design engineering course, and my role covered a lot of ground.

<!-- Video asset: 6a3b3a35-7f73-490d-89f9-973adb12f2b9 -->

I trained machine learning models and wrote Python scripts to automate the data labeling process. I also built scripts for recording, transcribing, and vectorizing the stories, and handled both the frontend (robot interaction) and backend (ML model and utilities).

I designed the circuit, soldered everything onto a protoboard, and sized the power supply to ensure the system could handle the setup.

<!-- Video asset: 114ac6a2-6515-4290-9c6f-19c0af5e21a3 -->

Apollo was a mix of storytelling, technology, and design coming together. It was exciting to see how much we achieved in such a short time, creating something that feels both meaningful and magical.

We were thrilled to take 3rd place in the 2024 InterPLAY Design competition, which was an exciting way to wrap up such a fast-paced project.
