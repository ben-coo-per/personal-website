During the December 2022 AI hype wave, I wanted to find a project that would allow me to experiment with some of the new exciting tools on the market. This was also during a time that my friends and I were challenging one-another in low-commitment games like Wordle. I loved using Wordle because it took very little time but still kept people in contact over a shared activity. However, it does not have enough creativity in it for my taste. This set me down the path of creating a game for my friends that could add a small shot of creativity during the week while still being low commitment.

For this, I built a Sveltekit web-app, using Supabase as my backend, and OpenAI’s ChatGPT & DALL-E to do all of the generation work. The game consisted of a weekly “Make the best monster” challenge for a selected group of users. At the end of the week, everyone would vote on their favorite.
