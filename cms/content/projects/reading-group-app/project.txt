Title: Reading Group App

----

Subtitle: lightweight app to manage readings and attendance

----

MainDescription: A group of friends and I have been meeting weekly for a reading group since April of 2020. Throughout this time, both the number of members and the typical meeting attendance has ebbed and flowed but a dedicated core remained. We wanted to find a way to both better communicate if we were planning on attending and keep track of what we were reading. Of course, my answer was to build an rsvp app.
